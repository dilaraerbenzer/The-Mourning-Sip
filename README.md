Golden Grids is a darkly whimsical bartender simulation game where your skills, intuition, and creativity are put to the ultimate test. Set in a gothic bar during a somber morning following a mourning ritual, you must craft the perfect drinks to impress your mysterious and exacting boss. ### Gameplay

- **Pour, mix, and garnish:** Combine ingredients like syrups, bitters, and condiments while managing glass volume and fractions.

- **Balance your stats:** Every ingredient affects the Sweet, Bitter, and Sour levels of your drink. Achieve the ideal mix to earn maximum praise.

- **Special rules:** Adding ice dilutes your drink but can earn bonus points. Condiments give extra flavour and small bonuses.

- **Trial by taste:** Your boss judges each drink based on closeness to the perfect mix as well as each ending type, rewarding skillful bartending.

- **Multiple endings:** From sweet, sour, and bitter to too little liquid or a perfect mix, each drink outcome is unique and accompanied by immersive lore.

### Features

- **Dynamic scoring:** Drinks are evaluated based on their distance from ideal flavour profiles.

- **Atmospheric storytelling:** Gothic endings match the gothic bar and mourning theme, making every drink a narrative experience.

- **Replayable:** Experiment with 26 different ingredients, 2 types of garnish and techniques to unlock different endings.

### Controls

- **Add ingredient:** Click/hold ingredients to add into the mixer.

- **Add ice:** Ice can only be added once per drink.

- **Add condiment:** Adds extra points and flavour, limited to one per drink.

- **Finalise drink:** Submit your creation for evaluation.
